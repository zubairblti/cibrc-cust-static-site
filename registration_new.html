Registration Guidelines

    Please follow the registration guidelines given below:
  • A single registration form is applicable for one paper and person only. Participants must pay registration fee for each paper separately.
  • Make sure to provide all the valid details for registration form carefully. Your name and title of the paper mentioned on registration form must match the details on the paper submitted, as information provided will be used for the publication of program card and certificate.
  • Before registration, kindly pay the registration fee along with providing the payment details. After registration you will receive an automatic response email. However, your registration will be confirmed after your payment verification.
  • Registration fee is non-refundable.
  • Once you have successfully made payments as per details provided on website. Kindly remember to send the scanned copies of online/ATM/Account Transfer receipts for registration confirmation. Cheque/Demand Draft/Pay Order need to be sent to the address mentioned on website. Kindly make sure that the scanned copies of the receipts are legible.
  • Walk-in registration is possible at the conference venue at 08:00am on Oct 19th, 2017.
